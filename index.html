MITx-Canvas LTI Link Updater

Drop in your old Canvas export (.imscc) and the new MITx course export (.tar.gz), in any order. This tool updates every MITx link to the new course and gives you an updated Canvas file to import, plus a report.
Drag both files here, or click to browse
Old Canvas export (.imscc) + new MITx export (.tar.gz)
Advanced: don’t have the new MITx export?

Only the Canvas export (.imscc) is needed. Use this only if the new MITx course is an exact copy of the old one (a Studio re-run or export/import copy — same block IDs). Links are not checked against the new course, so spot-check a couple of links after importing into Canvas.

Everything runs in your browser — your files never leave your computer.
